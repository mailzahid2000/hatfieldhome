Our Homes

Hatfield Home currently operates three residential homes providing a safe, nurturing and family-style environment for young people aged 8–17.

Our homes are designed to promote stability, emotional wellbeing, and personal development while ensuring young people feel supported and valued.

Home Environment

  • Comfortable private bedrooms
  • Shared living and dining spaces
  • Safe outdoor areas
  • Structured daily routines
  • Support with education and activities

Our Approach

We focus on creating a positive and structured home environment where young people are encouraged to build confidence, develop life skills, and engage positively with their community.
