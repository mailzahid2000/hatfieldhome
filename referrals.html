Referrals

Hatfield Home accepts referrals from Local Authorities and Social Workers. We carefully assess each referral to ensure the placement is suitable and beneficial for the young person.

Placement Criteria

  • Young people aged 8–17
  • Emotional and behavioural difficulties (EBD)
  • Require structured residential support
